About the Program

Make Change Count for Schools began in 2015, when founders Alex Wu and Sohan Subhash were inspired by a program known as Make Change Count, based in downtown San Diego, that collects donations to benefit the homeless using repurposed parking meters. They created Make Change Count for Schools (MCCFS), which today seeks to create an effective and convenient funding platform for local schools that involves active community participation. MCCFS, with help from local sponsors and San Diego-based parking technology company IPS Group, installs repurposed parking meters on school campuses at the administration’s discretion. We at MCCFS hope our program will provide a more creative, sustainable, and hassle-free fundraising option for local schools.

MCCFS is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization. Our Employer Identification Number (EIN) is 81-4421119.
